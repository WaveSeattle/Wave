Why Volunteer with WAVE?

Volunteering with WAVE is more than just giving your time—it's about becoming part of a family that supports families when they need it most.

Make a Real Impact

Your time and energy directly translate into support for families during their most challenging moments. Every hour you volunteer creates tangible change.

Join Our Community

Connect with like-minded individuals who share your passion for helping others. Build lasting friendships and be part of something bigger than yourself.

Flexible Opportunities

Whether you have 2 hours a week or 2 hours a month, we have volunteer opportunities that fit your schedule and interests.

Volunteer Opportunities

We offer diverse ways to get involved, from direct family support to behind-the-scenes event planning.

Family Support Volunteer

Time Commitment: 4-8 hours per week

Training Required: Yes (provided)

Work directly with families to provide emotional support, practical assistance, and companionship. This role involves visiting families, helping with errands, childcare, and being a listening ear during difficult times.

Event Planning Team

Time Commitment: 2-6 hours per week

Training Required: Orientation session

Help plan and execute our fundraising events, awareness campaigns, and community gatherings. From logistics coordination to creative planning, this team makes our events successful.

Administrative Support

Time Commitment: 3-5 hours per week

Training Required: Basic orientation
