Celebrating Our Achievements

Thanks to our amazing community, we've hosted numerous successful events that have made a real difference in the lives of children and families.

2024 Holiday Gala

Date: December 10, 2024

Attendance: 350 guests

Funds Raised: $125,000

Our most successful gala to date! The evening featured dinner, live entertainment, and a touching presentation from families whose lives have been transformed by our support. The funds raised will directly support 25 families in the coming year.

Fundraising Community

Summer Fun Run 2024

Date: August 15, 2024

Participants: 500+ runners

Funds Raised: $45,000

An incredible turnout for our summer fun run! Families, children, and community members came together for a beautiful morning of running, wellness activities, and community building. All proceeds went directly to our emergency assistance fund.

Health & Wellness Family-Friendly

Spring Awareness Campaign

Date: April 20, 2024

Reach: 10,000+ people

New Volunteers: 85 sign-ups

Our awareness campaign reached thousands through social media, community partnerships, and local media coverage. The campaign significantly increased volunteer registrations and community awareness about pediatric cancer support needs.

Winter Volunteer Training

Date: January 25, 2024

Volunteers Trained: 120

Training Hours: 480 total hours

Our comprehensive volunteer training session equipped new and returning volunteers with the skills and knowledge needed to provide the best support to families. The training covered emotional support, practical assistance, and organizational protocols.
