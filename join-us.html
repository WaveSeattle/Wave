How You Can Help

WAVE volunteers power our events, outreach, and fundraising—supporting pediatric cancer research and uncompensated care at Seattle Children’s.

  • Help run charity events and community booths
  • Coordinate sponsorships and local partnerships
  • Create content to raise awareness
  • Support families with resources and logistics

Volunteer Application Process

  1. Submit Application — Fill out the online volunteer form (link above). Answer the essay prompts thoughtfully.
  2. Application Review + Reference Check — Our team reviews your application and may contact references.
  3. Orientation Session + Training — Learn our mission, values, safety, and event procedures.
  4. Onboarding Process — Paperwork, scheduling, and welcome to the team!
