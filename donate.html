How Your Donation Helps

WAVE is a youth-led guild supporting pediatric cancer research and uncompensated care. We raise funds so researchers can develop better, safer treatments—and so families can focus on healing, not bills.

We donate to Seattle Children’s, where your gift powers clinical trials, lab equipment, and direct support for children in active treatment. Every contribution—large or small—moves us closer to a future where kids beat cancer faster and with fewer side effects.
