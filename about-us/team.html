Youth-Led Leadership

WAVE is proudly led by a dedicated team of young advocates who understand the power of collective action. Our leadership team brings together diverse perspectives, innovative ideas, and unwavering commitment to our cause.

Each team member contributes unique skills and experiences, from event planning and community outreach to digital marketing and fundraising strategy. Together, we work to ensure that every initiative we undertake maximizes impact for the children and families we serve.
