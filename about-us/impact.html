Real Impact, Real Stories

Our work goes beyond numbers. Every dollar raised, every hour volunteered, and every event hosted contributes to life-changing outcomes for children and families in our community.

Through our partnership with Seattle Children's Hospital, we've helped fund critical research projects that are developing new treatments for pediatric cancers. Additionally, our support for uncompensated care has ensured that families can focus on what matters most – their child's recovery – without the burden of financial stress.

Research Funding

Our contributions have supported 5 major research projects at Seattle Children's Hospital, including studies on new immunotherapy treatments and precision medicine approaches for pediatric cancers.

Uncompensated Care

We've helped cover treatment costs for over 150 families, ensuring that financial barriers don't prevent children from receiving the best possible care.

Community Awareness

Our events and campaigns have reached over 10,000 community members, raising awareness about pediatric cancer and the importance of research funding.

Looking Forward

As we continue to grow, our impact grows with us. We're committed to expanding our reach and deepening our support for the families and researchers working tirelessly to end pediatric cancer.
