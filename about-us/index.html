Our Mission

Who We Are

WAVE (Wellness, Awareness, Vision, Empowerment) is a youth-led organization dedicated to supporting cancer research and uncompensated care at Seattle Children's Hospital. Founded by passionate young advocates, we believe in the power of community action to create meaningful change in the lives of children and families facing cancer.

Through innovative fundraising events, awareness campaigns, and community engagement, we work tirelessly to bridge the gap between hope and healing. Our commitment extends beyond just raising funds – we aim to build a supportive community that understands the challenges faced by families dealing with pediatric cancer.
