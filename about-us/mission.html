Our Mission

Empowering communities to support children battling cancer through research funding and uncompensated care.

What Drives Us

At WAVE, we believe that every child deserves access to the best possible cancer care, regardless of their family's financial situation. Our mission is to bridge the gap between medical excellence and financial accessibility by supporting both groundbreaking research and uncompensated care at Seattle Children's Hospital.

We are committed to creating a world where a cancer diagnosis doesn't mean choosing between treatment and financial stability. Through our youth-led initiatives, we work to ensure that innovative treatments reach those who need them most.

Our Core Values

Wellness

Supporting holistic health and healing for children and families affected by cancer

Awareness

Educating communities about pediatric cancer and the importance of research funding

Vision

Envisioning a future where every child has access to life-saving cancer treatments

Empowerment

Empowering youth to become advocates and leaders in the fight against pediatric cancer

Our Approach

We take a multi-faceted approach to supporting pediatric cancer care:

Innovative Fundraising

Creating engaging events and campaigns that bring communities together while raising crucial funds for research and care.

Direct Support

Ensuring funds go directly to Seattle Children's Hospital to support both research initiatives and families in need.

Youth Leadership

Empowering young people to take leadership roles in advocacy and community mobilization.

Community Building

Creating lasting connections between families, supporters, and medical professionals.
